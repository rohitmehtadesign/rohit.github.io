Rohit Mehta

I’m a Principal Product Designer with 10+ years of experience designing complex, high-impact products across enterprise SaaS, fintech, hospitality, and consumer platforms. I currently design AI-enabled Supply Chain Management products at Oracle, modernizing legacy planning workflows using the Redwood Design System, while also contributing directly to the evolution of the design system itself in partnership with the core Redwood team. Previously, I worked on Oracle Marketing Cloud (Eloqua), designing scalable enterprise experiences used by global teams.

Earlier in my career, I led end-to-end design for large-scale, business-critical products at companies like Freshii, Compass Group Canada, and Ola. This included spearheading the complete redesign of Freshii’s mobile and web ordering platforms as a one-person design team, taking on an additional Product Manager role to lead initiatives across multiple Foodtastic brands, and driving high-impact, time-sensitive payments and fintech products at Ola. I’ve also led growth and loyalty squads, mentored designers, built and scaled design systems, and was a founding team member at Scandid, where I shaped the product from the ground up and pitched it on global startup platforms.
