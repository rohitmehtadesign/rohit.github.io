Rohit Mehta

Product Designer

I’m a Toronto-based Product Designer with 10 years of experience. Currently, I’ve been leading end-to-end Product Design at Freshii/Foodtastic since May 2021. My design experience encompasses early-stage startups, mid-sized enterprises, and large corporations, equipping me with a comprehensive understanding of the product design journey. At Freshii, I re-designed the core mobile apps and web ordering platforms and shipped them to have a positive impact on the business.


In the past, I’ve worked on consumer and enterprise products, at Compass Digital Labs, Flipp, Oracle, and Ola Cabs. I have had extensive experience working on complex projects of scale and zero-to-one projects. I’ve also had experience leading and facilitating design ceremonies, user testing, and design review sessions and workshops with a diverse group. I have experience working in agile setups and have experience working in squads, fostering close collaboration with stakeholders like Product Management, Engineering, Business partners, Marketing, and Data Science.
